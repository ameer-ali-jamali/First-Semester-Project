A HISTORY OF HELPING FAMILIES

In 1985, our founders were disturbed by high prices in the school uniform market adversely affecting families throughout the country. As a result, A-1 Uniform was born, providing better quality school uniforms than the competition at a great price point. Affordable school uniforms became available to everyone.
